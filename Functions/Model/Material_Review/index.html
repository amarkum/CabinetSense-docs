    Material Review in CabinetSense Software

    Summary or Overview

    This document covers the process of reviewing and verifying material assignments in cabinet designs using CabinetSense software. It provides detailed instructions on how to ensure accurate material assignments for your cabinet projects.

    How to

    Open your cabinet design in CabinetSense software. Navigate to the "Materials" tab or section. Review the material assignments for each component of the cabinet design. Verify that the materials assigned are correct and align with your design specifications. Make any necessary adjustments by selecting the component and choosing the correct material from the available options. Save your changes to update the material assignments in your cabinet design.

    FAQ

    How do I change the material of a cabinet component? To change the material of a cabinet component, select the component, navigate to the "Materials" tab, and choose the desired material from the available options. Can I create custom materials in CabinetSense software? Yes, you can create custom materials by importing texture images or specifying material properties within the software. Why are my material assignments not displaying correctly in the rendering? Ensure that the materials are applied correctly to the components in the design and that the rendering settings are configured to display materials accurately. Is there a way to batch assign materials to multiple components at once? Currently, CabinetSense software does not support batch assigning materials to multiple components simultaneously. Each component's material must be assigned individually. How can I see a list of all materials used in my cabinet design? You can view a list of all materials used in your cabinet design by accessing the "Materials" section and reviewing the material assignments for each component.

    Occasionally, material assignments may not display accurately in the 3D rendering due to rendering settings or software limitations. Ensure that materials are correctly assigned to components for optimal results. By following the instructions and tips provided in this document, you can effectively review and verify material assignments in your cabinet designs using CabinetSense software.
